About

Hello! I'm Lamya Qasem, a passionate graphic design student at George Brown College. My love for design and photography fuels my creativity, with nature being my constant muse. My ultimate goal is to become a renowned graphic designer and photographer, known for crafting visually captivating and thought-provoking works. I am proficient in Adobe Creative Suite, including Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign, which I use to bring my artistic visions to life. Additionally, I am fluent in Arabic and English, with basic proficiency in Chinese. This linguistic ability enables me to connect with diverse cultures, infusing my designs with a rich global perspective. Whether capturing moments through my lens or designing intricate visuals, I am dedicated to telling compelling stories that resonate universally.
